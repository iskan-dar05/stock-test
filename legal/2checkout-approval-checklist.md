# 2Checkout (Verifone) Approval Checklist

**Last Updated: [Date]**

This checklist outlines the requirements and best practices for successfully passing 2Checkout (Verifone) manual review for a subscription-based digital asset marketplace.

## Required Public Website Pages

### 1. Terms and Conditions Page
**Status:** ✅ Required  
**Location:** Must be accessible from footer and account pages  
**Requirements:**
- Clear subscription billing terms (monthly/yearly)
- First-month discount disclosure
- Download limits clearly stated (NO "unlimited" wording)
- Digital delivery explanation
- License terms and usage rights
- Refund policy reference
- Contact information

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Page is publicly accessible (no login required)
- [ ] Link in website footer
- [ ] Link in account/signup pages
- [ ] Last updated date visible
- [ ] All subscription terms clearly explained
- [ ] Download limits explicitly stated (e.g., "100 downloads per month")
- [ ] No "unlimited" or misleading language

### 2. Privacy Policy Page
**Status:** ✅ Required  
**Location:** Must be accessible from footer  
**Requirements:**
- Data collection disclosure
- 2Checkout payment processing mention
- Cookie policy
- User rights (GDPR/CCPA compliance)
- Contact information

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Page is publicly accessible
- [ ] Link in website footer
- [ ] 2Checkout mentioned as payment processor
- [ ] Data collection practices explained
- [ ] International user rights addressed
- [ ] Contact information for privacy inquiries

### 3. Refund Policy Page
**Status:** ✅ Required  
**Location:** Must be accessible from footer and checkout  
**Requirements:**
- Clear "no refunds" policy for digital products
- Exceptions clearly stated (billing errors, duplicates)
- Subscription cancellation terms
- 2Checkout compliance language
- Contact information for refund requests

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Page is publicly accessible
- [ ] Link in website footer
- [ ] Link visible during checkout process
- [ ] "No refunds for digital downloads" clearly stated
- [ ] Exceptions (billing errors) explained
- [ ] Subscription cancellation process described
- [ ] 2Checkout mentioned

### 4. Pricing Page
**Status:** ✅ Highly Recommended  
**Location:** Main navigation or footer  
**Requirements:**
- All subscription plans clearly displayed
- Monthly and yearly pricing shown
- First-month discount clearly marked
- Download limits per plan
- Billing cycle information
- "Auto-renewal" disclosure

**Checklist:**
- [ ] All subscription tiers visible
- [ ] Pricing in clear, prominent display
- [ ] Billing frequency (monthly/yearly) shown
- [ ] Promotional pricing clearly marked
- [ ] Download limits per plan displayed
- [ ] Auto-renewal mentioned
- [ ] Terms and refund policy linked

## Subscription Pricing Transparency Rules

### Pricing Display Requirements
- [ ] All prices clearly displayed before checkout
- [ ] Currency clearly indicated (USD, EUR, etc.)
- [ ] Billing frequency explicitly stated
- [ ] First-month discounts clearly marked as promotional
- [ ] Regular pricing shown alongside promotional pricing
- [ ] No hidden fees or charges
- [ ] Total cost calculation visible

### Auto-Renewal Disclosure
- [ ] Auto-renewal clearly disclosed on pricing page
- [ ] Auto-renewal mentioned during checkout
- [ ] Cancellation process explained
- [ ] Renewal date information provided
- [ ] Email notification of renewal mentioned

### Billing Cycle Clarity
- [ ] Monthly vs. yearly billing clearly differentiated
- [ ] Billing date information provided
- [ ] Proration policy explained (if applicable)
- [ ] Upgrade/downgrade terms clear

## Refund & Cancellation Disclosure

### Refund Policy Visibility
- [ ] Refund policy linked during checkout
- [ ] "No refunds" policy acknowledged during signup
- [ ] Checkbox or acknowledgment required before payment
- [ ] Refund policy accessible from account settings
- [ ] Cancellation process clearly explained

### Cancellation Process
- [ ] How to cancel clearly documented
- [ ] Cancellation accessible from account dashboard
- [ ] Effective date of cancellation explained
- [ ] No refund for remaining period clearly stated
- [ ] Access duration after cancellation explained

## Digital Delivery Explanation

### Delivery Method Disclosure
- [ ] "Digital delivery" clearly stated
- [ ] "No physical products" mentioned
- [ ] Immediate access after payment explained
- [ ] Download process described
- [ ] System requirements mentioned (if applicable)

### Delivery Confirmation
- [ ] Email confirmation sent after purchase
- [ ] Access instructions provided
- [ ] Support contact for delivery issues
- [ ] Delivery timeframe stated ("immediate" or "within X hours")

## Fair-Use Download Compliance

### Download Limits
- [ ] Download limits clearly stated (e.g., "100 downloads/month")
- [ ] NO "unlimited" wording used
- [ ] Limits per billing cycle explained
- [ ] Reset policy explained
- [ ] Fair use policy referenced

### Fair Use Policy
- [ ] Fair use policy clearly defined
- [ ] Excessive use consequences explained
- [ ] Account review process mentioned
- [ ] Limits enforced to ensure equitable access

### Language to Avoid
- ❌ "Unlimited downloads"
- ❌ "Unlimited access"
- ❌ "Download as much as you want"
- ✅ "100 downloads per month"
- ✅ "Up to 500 downloads per billing cycle"
- ✅ "Download limits apply per subscription tier"

## Common Rejection Reasons

### 1. Unclear Refund Policy
**Issue:** Vague or missing refund terms  
**Solution:**
- [ ] Explicit "no refunds" statement
- [ ] Exceptions clearly listed
- [ ] Policy accessible from multiple locations

### 2. Misleading Pricing
**Issue:** Hidden fees or unclear pricing  
**Solution:**
- [ ] All costs displayed upfront
- [ ] No surprise charges
- [ ] Promotional pricing clearly marked

### 3. Unclear Subscription Terms
**Issue:** Auto-renewal not disclosed  
**Solution:**
- [ ] Auto-renewal mentioned prominently
- [ ] Cancellation process explained
- [ ] Renewal date information provided

### 4. "Unlimited" Claims
**Issue:** Using "unlimited" for digital services  
**Solution:**
- [ ] Remove all "unlimited" language
- [ ] Use specific download limits
- [ ] Implement fair use policies

### 5. Missing Legal Pages
**Issue:** Terms, Privacy, or Refund policy missing  
**Solution:**
- [ ] All three pages present and accessible
- [ ] Links in footer
- [ ] Pages are complete and professional

### 6. Poor Website Quality
**Issue:** Unprofessional appearance or functionality  
**Solution:**
- [ ] Professional design
- [ ] Working links and navigation
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design
- [ ] Clear contact information

### 7. Insufficient Business Information
**Issue:** Missing company details  
**Solution:**
- [ ] Business name and address
- [ ] Contact email and phone
- [ ] Business registration information (if required)
- [ ] About page with company information
