# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: [Date]**

## 1. General Policy

StocksOcean operates as a digital asset marketplace providing subscription-based access to downloadable digital content. Due to the immediate digital delivery nature of our services, we maintain a strict no-refund policy except in limited circumstances as outlined below.

## 2. No Refunds for Digital Downloads

### 2.1 Digital Product Nature
All assets available on the Platform are digital products delivered immediately upon download. Once a digital asset is downloaded, it cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense.

### 2.2 No Refunds for Downloads
We do not provide refunds for:
- Individual asset downloads
- Assets accessed through subscription plans
- Assets that have been downloaded, even if unused
- Assets that do not meet your expectations
- Assets that are incompatible with your intended use

## 3. Subscription Refunds

### 3.1 No Refunds for Used Subscription Periods
Subscription fees are non-refundable once the subscription period has begun, regardless of:
- Number of downloads used
- Level of Platform usage
- Satisfaction with available content
- Technical issues resolved after subscription start
- Change of mind or circumstances

### 3.2 Subscription Cancellation
- You may cancel your subscription at any time
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- No refunds are provided for the remaining subscription period
- You retain access to the Platform until the end of your paid period
- You may continue downloading assets within your plan limits until cancellation takes effect

### 3.3 Free Trial Periods
If you cancel during a free trial period:
- No charges will be applied
- Access will terminate immediately upon cancellation
- No refund is necessary as no payment was processed

## 4. Exceptions and Refund Eligibility

### 4.1 Billing Errors
We will provide refunds for:
- Duplicate charges for the same subscription
- Charges for subscriptions you did not authorize
- Incorrect billing amounts due to technical errors
- Charges processed after account cancellation

**To request a refund for billing errors:**
- Contact us within 30 days of the charge
- Provide transaction details and explanation
- Allow 5-10 business days for investigation and processing

### 4.2 Duplicate Charges
If you are charged multiple times for the same subscription:
- Contact us immediately with transaction details
- We will investigate and refund duplicate charges
- Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days

### 4.3 Unauthorized Charges
If you believe a charge was unauthorized:
- Contact us immediately
- We will investigate the charge
- If confirmed unauthorized, a full refund will be issued
- We may require additional verification for security purposes

### 4.4 Technical Failures
In cases of significant Platform technical failures preventing access:
- We will work to resolve issues promptly
- Extended outages may result in service credits or extensions
- Refunds are not automatically provided for temporary technical issues
- Contact support if you experience persistent access problems

## 5. Refund Request Process

### 5.1 How to Request a Refund
To request a refund for an eligible exception:
1. Contact our support team at support@stocksocean.com
2. Provide your account email and transaction details
3. Explain the reason for your refund request
4. Include any relevant documentation or screenshots

### 5.2 Refund Review
- All refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis
- We will respond to refund requests within 5 business days
- Additional information may be requested for verification
- Refund decisions are final

### 5.3 Refund Processing
If your refund is approved:
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method
- Processing time: 5-10 business days
- You will receive email confirmation when the refund is processed
- Refunds may take additional time to appear in your account depending on your financial institution

## 6. Chargebacks and Disputes

### 6.1 Chargeback Policy
If you initiate a chargeback with your payment provider:
- Your account will be immediately suspended
- We will investigate the chargeback claim
- We may provide evidence to your payment provider
- Chargebacks for valid subscriptions may result in account termination

### 6.2 Dispute Resolution
We encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback:
- We can often resolve issues more quickly through direct communication
- Chargebacks may result in additional fees and account restrictions
- We are committed to resolving legitimate concerns fairly

## 7. 2Checkout (Verifone) Compliance

### 7.1 Payment Processor Requirements
This Refund Policy complies with 2Checkout (Verifone) requirements for:
- Clear disclosure of refund terms
- Digital product refund policies
- Subscription cancellation procedures
- Billing error resolution

### 7.2 2Checkout Dispute Process
If a dispute is filed through 2Checkout:
- We will respond according to 2Checkout's dispute resolution process
- We may provide transaction evidence and documentation
- 2Checkout will make a final determination based on their policies

## 8. Subscription Modifications

### 8.1 Plan Upgrades
If you upgrade your subscription plan:
- The upgrade takes effect immediately
- You are charged the prorated difference
- No refunds are provided for the previous plan period
- The new billing cycle begins on the upgrade date

### 8.2 Plan Downgrades
If you downgrade your subscription plan:
- The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- No refunds are provided for the current period
- You retain access to your current plan until the period ends

## 9. Special Circumstances

### 9.1 Account Termination by Us
If we terminate your account for violation of Terms:
- No refunds are provided
- Access is terminated immediately
- Outstanding fees remain due

### 9.2 Platform Discontinuation
In the unlikely event we discontinue the Platform:
- We will provide advance notice to subscribers
- Refunds may be provided for unused subscription periods
- Specific terms will be communicated at that time

## 10. Consumer Rights

### 10.1 Statutory Rights
This policy does not affect your statutory rights as a consumer. If you are located in a jurisdiction that provides mandatory refund rights (such as the European Union's 14-day cooling-off period for distance contracts), those rights apply where legally required.

### 10.2 EU Distance Selling Rights
For EU consumers:
- Digital content delivered immediately with your consent waives the 14-day right of withdrawal
- By subscribing and downloading content, you consent to immediate delivery
- This waiver is in compliance with EU Directive 2011/83/EU
