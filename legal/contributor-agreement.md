## 5. Royalty System

### 5.1 Royalty Calculation
You earn royalties based on:
- Number of downloads of your assets
- Asset price per download
- Your contributor level (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum)
- Royalty percentage associated with your level

### 5.2 Contributor Levels and Royalty Rates
Royalty percentages vary by contributor level:
- **Bronze Level:** 40% of asset price per download
- **Silver Level:** 45% of asset price per download
- **Gold Level:** 50% of asset price per download
- **Platinum Level:** 55% of asset price per download

### 5.3 Level Requirements
Contributor levels are determined by:
- Total number of assets uploaded
- Total earnings generated
- Asset quality and performance
- Platform participation and compliance
- Other factors as determined by StocksOcean

### 5.4 Royalty Modifications
We reserve the right to:
- Modify royalty rates and level requirements
- Adjust contributor levels based on performance

**Notice:** Significant changes to royalty rates will be communicated at least 30 days in advance.

### 5.5 Per-Download Royalties
Royalties are calculated and paid:
- On a per-download basis
- Only for completed downloads by paying subscribers
- Not for preview views or incomplete downloads
- According to your contributor level at the time of download
